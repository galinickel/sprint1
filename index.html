Welcome to Minesweeper!

You may choose one of the three difficulty levels and test your skills!

Instructions:

  • In order to win the game, you must flag all the mines on the board and reveal every single cell that isn't a mine.
  • If you step on a mine, you lose a life. Your lives are represented by the red balloons above the board.
  • In order to flag cells, click them with your right mouse-button. You can undo the process by clicking them with your right mouse-button a second time.

Helpful Tools:

  • A hint system is available. It will show the chosen cell's neighbors for a limited time so you can plan your next move.
  • A safe-click system is available. It will show you a place on the board that is guaranteed to be free of mines.
  • Please note that these special tools are of limited use! You can track your usage of them in the upper left corner of the screen.


Don't forget to have fun!
